The student talked about her career in webcam business

Hello! My name is Maria. I am a 4th year university student and a webcam model. I want to tell you how I manage to combine webcam and study, how I got into this industry and talk a little about my earnings.

“After graduation, I plan to remain a model”

For almost a year and a half I have been working as a webcam model at home. It all started with the usual registration on the site, I created my account, set up the technique and after a few days I launched the first stream. I do not want people from the university to know about my hobbies, so I have to work in a mask to hide my face. Webcam for me is a good source of income. After graduation, I plan to remain a model and I can spend more time on streams.

“Webcam for female students is a good idea”

Because of my studies, I can only broadcast in the evening. I can spend 3-4 hours on the stream, but sometimes I can work until late at night, when the audience is good and craves for the spectacle.
In my opinion, webcam for female students is a good idea, because each model can work in free time.

“Earnings are $ 1500-3000 per month”

I earn less than popular models earn, but in their chat rooms they spend a lot of time and carefully prepare their streams. I have no such opportunity, but I try very hard and often change my images. My salary is $1500-3000 per month. Of course, I would like to earn more.

“Webcam students come a lot”

Female students come to webcam a lot and many use webcam as their main source of income. When I started working as a model, I met a girl who turned out to be a college student. She has now completed her education and is fully immersed in the webcam industry. If you also want to become a webcam model, everything is in your hands, as they say. Good luck to all!
